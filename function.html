index.html

Habits List

You are welcomed by your habits list, list that's kept forever in your browser's local memory.

On your habits list, next to your hobby's name, you can click the progress button to generate the number input textbox, in order to add time to your hobby whcih has not been tracked using browser's timers.html.

Next to the habit's progress button is a trash icon button. Click it to simply delete the hobby and its' recorded data.

Sort Hobbies

Using history.html's history sort feature I designed this: a better way to sort your hobbies if you have ADHD a variety of beautiful interests.

You can either gray out the items at the bottom of the list/those that do not fit your criteria, or hide them entirely.

Add New Hobby

To create a new habit, click on the text field below your hobbies' list and input your new hobby's name, as well as the frequency.

Four options are available:

  • Daily
  • Weekly: Choose the days on which you want to do this hobby.
  • Monthly: This is a feature designed for me, but you can use it too. Pick between odd, even days, or select a singular date on which you want to do your hobby.

Done? Then click the add habit button.

Clear All Button

The "Delete All Habits" or clear all button allows you to delete all your inputed habits, along their progress history.

Do note: Recordings older than 30 days are automatically deleted. If you worry about clutter, just let a month pass by, and you can keep your hobbies.

If you clear your browser's cookies for neocities, your hobbies will all be deleted.

timers.html

Timer Section

From the dropdown menu, select the hobby you want to track, then click start timer.

You can exit the app freely, as long as your browser has the permission to run in the background. Your progress will still be tracked. (still testing this)
